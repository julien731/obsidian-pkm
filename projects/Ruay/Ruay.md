# Ruay

Ruay (รวย — Thai for "rich") is a personal finance app that combines zero-dollar budgeting with the envelope system, adapted for modern digital banking. The goal is to help people understand how much money they have, where it is, and take control of their finances to build wealth.

Formerly codenamed "Thrifty." Brand selected 2026-03-31. See [[projects/Ruay/Brand Naming Research]] for the full naming process.
