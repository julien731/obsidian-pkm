# Questions

- Backlog Management
    - How well does this Product Manager keep the backlog groomed and up-to-date? #excellence
    - How well are goals defined for each sprint? #excellence
    - Are the user stories comprehensive, clear, and actionable? #excellence
- Communication
    - How effective is the Product Manager’s communication internally with the team? #excellence
    - How clear and understandable are the documents produced (e.g., product documentation, meeting notes, etc)? #excellence
- Getting Things Done
    - Does the Product Manager gets things done on time (e.g., writing user stories, grooming the backlog, gathering requirements, preparing documentation, etc)? #ownership
    - How good is the Product Manager at finding solutions to problems simple and complex? #ownership
- Technical Knowledge
    - How well do you feel the product Manager understands the technical challenges and complexities that the Engineering team is facing? #excellence
    - Does the Product Manager comes up with ideas and propositions to address technical challenges? #excellence
- Processes
    - How well does the Product Manager understands and follows our established processes? #excellence
- Product
    - How well does the Product Manager explains their reasoning for product decisions and prioritization? #excellence
    - Do you think that the Product Manager takes the right decisions with product features and prioritization? #ownership
- Trust
    - Do you feel like the Product Manager has your back when dealing with clients’ requirements and expectations? #courage
    - Do you feel comfortable bringing up issues and problems to the Product Manager? #dependable
    - Do you feel like the Product Manager supports you when you need it? #dependable
- Leadership
    - Does the Product Manager display a positive attitude to the team and the clients? #excellence
    - Do you think that the Product Manager is sharing the right information at the right time with the Engineering team? #excellence
