## Summary

The HFT Guy figured out how to run gigabit ethernet over existing phone wires using German adapters from GigaCopper. Phone wires work much better than power lines (less electrical noise), but almost no manufacturers make products for this use case.

- UK homes are full of phone sockets but have zero ethernet wiring
