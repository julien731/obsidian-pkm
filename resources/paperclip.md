[Paperclip](https://github.com/paperclipai/paperclip) is an open-source AI agent orchestration platform. It coordinates multiple agents (e.g. Claude Code, Cursor, OpenClaw) to autonomously handle business operations, with built-in governance, budgeting, task management, and multi-tenancy support. Self-hosted on Node.js + PostgreSQL.
