# Top 7 Best Vacation Rental Channel Managers in 2021 (Comparisons & Reviews)


- [**Uplisting**](https://www.uplisting.io/?via=floorspace) – **Best overall — our top pick.** Comprehensive short-term rental management software platform. User-friendly, tons of features, great support, and a premium price tag.
- [**Smartbnb**](https://www.smartbnb.io/?afmc=floorspace&utm_campaign=floorspace&utm_source=leaddyno&utm_medium=affiliate) – **Best for basic channel sync & automation.** Easy to set up, easy to use, excellent channel sync and automation features, and an affordable price.
- [**OwnerRez**](https://www.ownerreservations.com/?referredBy=oru5b14b4bf41x) **– Best value for property management features.** Owner commission statements and payouts, automation features, great customer support, and flexible, affordable pricing.
- [**iGMS](https://www.igms.com/?afmc=9u)** – **Best advanced feature set.** Comprehensive short-term rental management software platform. Tons of advanced features, and a flexible entry-level pricing level.

- [**Syncbnb](http://www.syncbnb.com/a/floorspace) – Best calendar and rate syncing features.** Offers the most channels by far (200+ native, real-time integrations), and a ‘Zero Double-Booking Guarantee.’
- [**Lodgify](https://www.lodgify.com/?afmc=u2) – Best direct booking website feature**. Manage all your channels, including website bookings, in one convenient dashboard.
