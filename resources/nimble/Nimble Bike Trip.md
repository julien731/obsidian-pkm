# Nimble Bike Trip

# Required

The following items are required to ensure a safe and pleasant trip. It will also ensure that everyone is road-legal in case we come across a police checkpoint.

- Valid driver’s license
- Up-to-date bike registration
- Up-to-date road tax (Pasi)
- Up-to-date compulsory insurance (Por Ror Bor)
- Proper helmet

# Recommended

- Motorbike gloves
- Proper shoes, no sandals
