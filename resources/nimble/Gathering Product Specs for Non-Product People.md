# Gathering Product Specs for Non-Product People

- What problem are we trying to solve? This is extremely important. When sharing specs and requirements, people can be confusing and sometimes not focus on the right areas. It is then easy to misunderstand the requirements.
However, understanding the problem that we are trying to solve will, in most cases, help drive the product discovery, and it will help pinpoint where our understanding doesn't necessarily make sense.
- Data
    - Where does it come from?
    - Where does it go?
