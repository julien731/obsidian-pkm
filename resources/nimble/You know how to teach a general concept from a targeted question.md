# You know how to teach a general concept from a targeted question

Area: Mentorship (../Product%20Track%20Areas/Mentorship%206f3d861fd3ec4628a27068e951398c78.md)

When someone more junior asks a question about a project/feature/process/etc you should give them the answer, **and** you should also give them a higher-level explanation that they can apply and re-use in all similar situations in the future.
