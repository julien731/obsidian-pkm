# One-on-One Meeting Structure

The one-on-one meetings should be scheduled for 45 minutes. The 15 minutes to complete the hour can then be used to either write down the meeting summary, observations, and commitments â€”if anyâ€” or, if necessary, to expand on a lengthy topic.
