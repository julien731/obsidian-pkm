# People Ops Interview

- What do you do in your free time?
- Are you a foodie?
- What kind of events have you organized?
- How late do you stay in the events?
- Introvert or extrovert?
- How do you deal with a team of introverts?
- How do you deal with rejection?
- How would you manage people who have a longer tenure than you do?
- How do you feel about working OT from time to time? E.g., events.
- Labor law? E.g., CCTV can we legally install unilaterally?
- How would you handle a situation when an employee gets caught working somewhere else?
- How would you approach training/coaching in a technical environment?
