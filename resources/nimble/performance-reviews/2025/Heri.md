# 360 Review: Heri (2025)

## Summary

**Strengths:**
- Responsive and accessible (4 reviewers) — quick to answer questions and unblock the team
- Proactive product improvement (2) — actively suggests UX enhancements and new tools like Microsoft Clarity
- Open to team input (2) — receptive to developer suggestions and feedback
- Clear requirements and realistic expectations (1) — structured stories with minimal ambiguity, no unnecessary pressure

**Areas for improvement:**
- AI-generated ticket quality (3) — tickets created with AI lack verification, can be unclear, overly lengthy, or confusing for developers
- Acknowledgment of comments (1) — team unsure if their questions on stories have been seen; even a quick emoji reaction would help

## What Could Be Better

### From S M Abu Taher

- Shortcut story descriptions could be more detailed, especially for new joiners on the team who need more context.

### From Dang Phuong Nam

- It would be really helpful if you could acknowledge when you've seen our comments or questions on user stories, even with a quick emoji reaction or a short reply. Sometimes it's unclear if a comment has been seen, which can lead to us following up on Slack just to confirm you're aware of it.

### From Khanh

At the moment, I don’t see any major gaps that need improvement. He’s performing strongly in both execution and collaboration

### From Suphanat Jarukulgowit

While using AI to generate tickets is very helpful, the context in some tickets can sometimes be unclear, overly lengthy, or not direct enough.

To improve this, it would be beneficial to ensure that tickets are more concise and structured — for example, clearly stating the objective, scope, and expected outcome at the beginning.
