# 360 Review: Jack (2025)

## Summary

**Strengths:**
- Proactive and always ahead (4 reviewers) — anticipates needs, prepares documentation, reaches out to clients for clarification before being asked
- Clear communication and task breakdown (4) — translates complex information into clear, developer-friendly stories with proper context
- Strong client management (3) — balances expectations with team capacity, pushes back professionally on unclear or unrealistic requests
- Calm under pressure (2) — makes urgent situations feel manageable, maintains composure
- Reliable and organized (2) — never forgets things, keeps projects under control

**Areas for improvement:**
- Sprint readiness with external dependencies (1) — tickets sometimes pending client decisions after sprint start; preparing them earlier would help
- Earlier QA on tickets (1) — doing QA sooner on "Ready for QA" tickets would catch issues earlier and reduce release builds
- Frustration management (1) — the shared complaint list about a client PM was counterproductive; needs better channels for frustrations
- Alignment with stakeholders (1) — could communicate more with certain stakeholders to reduce feature misalignment

## What Could Be Better

### From Zaw Myo Naing

Some constructive feedback points and opportunities for improvement

- Sprint readiness: For the JBNA project, there were times when we had to wait for the client’s decision/response on the sprint goal or other external dependencies. In those situations, he made sure the team was not blocked and that we could start with what we had. Where possible, having tickets and tasks prepared before the sprint planning meeting could have provided better visibility into the sprint.

- Tickets QA: He ensured that release builds were QA-ed before they were published, which prevented issues from reaching clients or users. Where possible, doing QA earlier on the tickets marked "Ready for QA" could help catch issues sooner and reduce the need for additional release builds and the time involved in preparing them.
