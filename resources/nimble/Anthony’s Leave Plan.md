# Anthony’s Leave Plan

# Long Sick Leave

- Employees can request as many sick leave days as they need. The employer is only required to pay 30 work days.
- A medical certificate can be asked (although not mandatory) because the leave is more than 3 days.
- What’s left on the sick leave policy needs to be paid (up to 30 days).

## Requirements for SSO Benefits

- Submit the `สปส.2-01` form.
    
    [27.pdf](Anthony%E2%80%99s%20Leave%20Plan/27.pdf)
    
- Medical certificate.
    - The doctor must recommend a rest duration.
- Employment letter.
- Passport.
- Sick leave policy status report.
- Bank book (SCB, KBank, BBL, KTB, KMA, TTB, CIMB, Islam Bank).

### Benefits

- 50% of salary capped at 15,000 THB per month (paid by Thailand’s social security).
- Three months maximum.
