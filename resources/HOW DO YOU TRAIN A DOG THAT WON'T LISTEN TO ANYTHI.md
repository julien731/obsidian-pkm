# HOW DO YOU TRAIN A DOG THAT WON'T LISTEN TO ANYTHING!


- Call "heel" when he walks next to you
- Again: directional changes
- Apply pressure to correct the behavior when he doesn't follow
    - Slip leash is necessary
    - Prong collar is still used in this video
