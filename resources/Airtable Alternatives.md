# Notes

## nocodb

- ❌ Does not display the relationship entry names. Will not be fixed. See https://github.com/nocodb/nocodb/issues/7053.
- Great Airtable import.

## apitable

[Automate Data Across 6,000+ Apps](https://aitable.ai/)

- Saas available at [https://aitable.ai/](https://aitable.ai/)
- No Airtable import.
- Multiple views.
- Forms.

## Rowy

[Rowy - Low-code backend on Google Cloud and Firebase](https://www.rowy.io/)

- ❌ No currency field type.

## Teable

[Teable - Postgres-Airtable Fusion](https://teable.io/)

- No Airtable import.
- Only 3 views (grid, Kanban, form).
- Can connect external Postgres DB.

## baserow

[Baserow - Open source no-code database](https://baserow.io/)

- Good Airtable import.
- Lots of field types.
- ❌ No currency field type.
- Limited views available.
