# Northworks Video Content Playbook

Playbook for getting maximum long-term value from each short-form video (Instagram reel, TikTok) posted for Northworks. Short-form social content is ephemeral by default — this flow converts each video into a permanent, indexable, multi-channel asset. Context: the northworks.asia site only has 12 URLs in its sitemap and growing the indexable surface is a priority.

## The Flow

For each new video produced:

1. **Native uploads (same content, three platforms)**
   - Instagram Reel
   - TikTok
   - YouTube Short
   Native uploads perform far better than cross-posted links on every algorithm.

2. **Blog post on northworks.asia**
   - Embed the **YouTube** version (not IG or TikTok — those embeds are heavy, iframed, and break if the source is removed)
   - Include the full transcript
   - Short written takeaway/commentary above or below the embed
   - Add `VideoObject` schema markup so the post is eligible for Google video results
   - Internal link from any relevant service page (e.g., a video about leadership → `/services/hr-leadership-consulting/`)

3. **LinkedIn native post**
   - Rework the transcript into a standalone text post (not a link share — native reach is ~10× higher)
   - Optionally upload the video natively too

4. **Quarterly lead magnet**
   - Every 3 months, bundle 10–15 videos + transcripts into a themed PDF or gated page
   - Email-gated for lead capture

## Why Each Step

| Step | Payoff |
|------|--------|
| YouTube Short | 2nd largest search engine; videos stay discoverable for years; free distribution |
| Blog post + embed | Turns ephemeral social into permanent indexable pages; directly addresses the northworks.asia indexing/content-depth gap |
| VideoObject schema | Rich results in Google SERPs; compounds organic visibility |
| Service-page embeds | Higher dwell time and topical relevance on commercial pages |
| LinkedIn native | Reaches the B2B audience directly; different people than IG/TikTok |
| Quarterly lead magnet | Converts passive viewers into email subscribers |

## Defaults & Decisions

- **Blog embeds**: always YouTube, never IG/TikTok
- **Transcription**: Julien is happy to transcribe (not a bottleneck)
- **Schema**: `VideoObject` on every video blog post — non-negotiable
- **Internal linking**: each video blog must link out to at least one relevant service page
