# Hua Hin Airbnb Requirements


## Non-Negotiables

- Modern house, ideally a new construction
- 3 bedrooms, 2-3 bathrooms
- 120 sqm+
- Swimming pool
- Inside a recent mooban
- Enclosed property
- Parking space

## Open to Offers

- Location, as long as it remains within a 15-minutes radius of Hua Hin center
- Some green/garden area
- Not too close to neighboring houses
- Close to 7-Eleven

## Budget

4,000,000-5,500,000 THB. The lower the better.
