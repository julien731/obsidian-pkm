# Expectations

- Startup work. Like when I started at Appsflyer.
- Flexible working hours.
- Remote.
- Same salary (or more).
- International.
- Flexible vacations.
- Good insurance.
- Good benefits.

- Work with smart people.
- Clear career path.
- Monday → Friday schedule.

## NOT

- Going to the office 5 days a week.
- Little vacation days (< 20 days is a deal-breaker).
- Micro-managed.
- Traditional Thai company.
- Unclear hierarchy.
- OT.
- Poor work-life balance.
