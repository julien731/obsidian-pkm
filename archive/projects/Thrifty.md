## Core Metrics

---

Thrifty lives by three core metrics. Those metrics are everything. It's what defines success, and it's what defines who we are.

1. **Negative-to-positive-net-worth:** did our users get financially better after starting to use Thrifty?
2. **Positive net-worth:** are our users financially secure?
3. **Goals achievement:** are our users able to reach their financial goals?
