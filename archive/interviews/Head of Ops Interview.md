# Head of Ops Interview

# Mindset

- You’ve recently sold Workmate where you had shares. What kind of work/life balance are you looking for now? Are you willing to work with intensity?
    - 
- What do you *really* want to do?

# Skills

- You’ve worked in a number of different fields. What are you best at? What are your weaker areas?
    - Business dev partnerships
    - Strategy and planning
    - Optimization, setting up processes, SOPs.
    - Strong BD network. Can be revenue generating.
- You’re now an advisor at GoWabi. What have you achieved in this role? Examples.
    - Done it for 10 years.
    - LINE deals was from him.
    - Sales funnel. Sales team. He brought the head of sales.
    - 40-50 customers since May.
    - Valuation game.
- If you join us, what would you need to learn?
    - Software development & delivery.
    - It’s important for all our leaders to be technical, at least to an extent. How would you define your technical level?
- You’ve been running large teams. What challenges did you face? Were they the same challenges you faced when you had 40 people (WearYouWant) and 70 people (Workmate)?
- Workmate: pivoted the business model and launched a new market strategy. Can you tell me more about this?
- What do you see as potential challenges with having a distributed company (Bangkok, HCMC, Da Nang)?

# Planning

- If you joined us, what would you do in the first 90 day? 180 days?
    - 30 days understand, sales, ops, delivery, pre-sales
    - 90 start building commercial, account management, build a stronger foundation to free leadership
    - 
- What is your long-term vision?
