# Aomâ€™s Interview

Summary

This meeting featured a presentation on enterprise marketing strategy, competitive positioning, and implementation planning, followed by a Q&A discussion that evaluated the presenter's experience and resource requirements.

### Competitive Positioning Analysis

- The presenter shared research on the company's positioning in the mid-to-high price project range
- Opportunity identified to prioritize comprehensive, inclusive partnerships offering holistic support and strategic guidance
- Competitive analysis included research on competitors' pricing and positioning
- Mystery shopping was identified as the most effective method for accurate competitive analysis

### Marketing Strategy Components

- Four key concepts highlighted, with executive engagement as the primary focus
- Seven-step process defined from planning to execution and optimization
- Strategy includes defining target audiences, content, channels, and budgets
- Primary channels include:
    - Offline industry events and conferences
    - Social media (especially LinkedIn and Facebook)
    - SEO and website blog content
    - PR and linking campaigns

### Event Marketing Approach

- Discussion of both attending industry events and hosting in-house events
- In-house events proposed twice monthly
- Previous experience with events like HRTEC discussed
- Recommendation for high-profile speakers at first in-house event to create buzz
- Networking opportunities emphasized as important for attendee value
- Strategic partnerships suggested to reduce costs and expand reach

### Content Strategy

- Value-driven content ecosystem proposed with industry-related keywords
- Content personalization recommended for different stages of customer journey
- Success stories and case studies highlighted as important content types
- Suggestion for short videos to summarize success stories and journey
- Need for consistent thought leadership content to build brand authority

### Resource Requirements

- Presenter estimated 24 days allocated for plan execution
- Discussion about required support:
    - Graphic designer resources needed
    - Content creation requiring approximately 1-2 days per piece
    - Need for industry insights from other team members
- Possibility of utilizing AI for content creation discussed

### Action Items

- [ ]  Implement social media monitoring for brand feedback
- [ ]  Develop content assets based on initial event themes
- [ ]  Create website enhancements to reflect key brand positioning
- [ ]  Explore co-marketing opportunities with complementary organizations
- [ ]  Establish process for consistent content creation (3 posts per week for Facebook)
- [ ]  Allocate resources for graphic design support
