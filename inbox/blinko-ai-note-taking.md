# Blinko — AI-Powered Note Taking App

**URL:** https://blinko.space/
**Docs:** https://docs.blinko.space/

## What It Is

Blinko is an open-source, self-hosted note-taking app built around quick card-style captures. It uses AI-driven RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to enable natural language search across your notes.

## Key Features

- **AI-Enhanced Search** — RAG-powered natural language search and retrieval
- **Data Ownership** — Fully self-hosted; you control your data
- **Fast Capture** — Plain text and Markdown support for quick idea capture
- **Open Source** — Community-driven, transparent codebase

## Why It Might Be Interesting

- Self-hosted alternative to cloud note apps
- AI search built-in without third-party data sharing
- Polished UI based on community feedback
- Could complement or replace part of current PKM setup
